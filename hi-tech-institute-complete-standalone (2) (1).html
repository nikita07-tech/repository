Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our programs, admissions, and more

Placement

What is Sunstone's placement opportunity program?

Our placement opportunity program guarantees comprehensive career support and placement opportunities. If you don't receive the promised opportunities, we offer first year tuition fee back.

Success

How does Sunstone ensure student success and career growth?

Through dedicated placement officers, personalized career counseling, skill development workshops, professional portfolio building, continuous mentorship, and access to extensive alumni network. We maintain 95% placement rate with comprehensive career support.

Admission

What are the admission requirements?

Admission requirements vary by program. Generally, you need minimum academic qualifications (45-50% marks), entrance exam scores, and completion of application process. Specific requirements are detailed in each program section.

Fees

Are there scholarship opportunities available?

Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships up to 100% tuition fee waiver through SAT (Sunstone Admission Test) and Bright Minds Scholarship program. Financial assistance and flexible payment options are also available.

Industry

What makes Sunstone's curriculum industry-relevant?

Our curriculum is designed by industry experts from Fortune 500 companies, includes live projects, real-world case studies, internships, and continuous updates based on industry trends and recruiter feedback.

Specializations

Can I change my specialization during the program?

Limited flexibility exists for specialization changes within the first semester, subject to availability and academic committee approval. We recommend choosing carefully during admission counseling.

Support

What support services are available for students?

We provide 24/7 learning support, academic counseling, career guidance, mental health support, library access, technical support, and dedicated student success managers for personalized assistance.

Technology

What technology platforms do you use for learning?

We use advanced Learning Management System (LMS), virtual labs, industry-standard software, AI-powered learning tools, video conferencing platforms, and mobile apps for seamless learning experience.

Assessment

How are students evaluated and assessed?

Continuous assessment through projects, assignments, presentations, practical exams, peer reviews, industry evaluations, and final examinations ensure comprehensive skill development and knowledge retention.

Alumni

Do you have an active alumni network?

Yes, our alumni network spans across 500+ companies globally. We organize regular networking events, mentorship programs, and career guidance sessions connecting current students with successful alumni.

Campus

What facilities are available on campus?

State-of-the-art infrastructure including modern classrooms, computer labs, library, auditorium, sports facilities, cafeteria, hostel accommodation, medical center, and recreational areas for holistic development.

International

Are there international exposure opportunities?

Yes, we offer international study tours, student exchange programs, global internships, international conferences, and partnerships with foreign universities for enhanced global exposure and career prospects.
